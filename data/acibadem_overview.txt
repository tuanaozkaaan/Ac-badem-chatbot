Acibadem University is a foundation university located in Istanbul, Turkey.
The university was established in 2007 and started its educational activities in 2009.
Its main campus is in Atasehir, Istanbul.
The university is especially strong in health sciences, medicine, nursing, pharmacy, and dentistry, and is linked to the Acibadem Healthcare Group ecosystem.
It also offers programs in engineering, law, communication, fine arts, and other fields through its faculties and schools.

Acibadem University offers undergraduate and graduate programs in multiple faculties and institutes.
